## Focus
- Optimize for multi-tenant safety, predictable releases, and observability.
- Prefer incremental delivery with feature flags and migration safety.
- Keep onboarding, self-serve UX, and supportability high.

## Decision lens
- Reliability before novelty.
- Backward compatibility for APIs and billing flows.
- Clear telemetry for activation, retention, and incident triage.
